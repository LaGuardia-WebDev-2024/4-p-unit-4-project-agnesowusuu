Braids, Cornrows, 4C, & The Beauty of it All:



Cornrows are more than just a hairstyle. By giving enslaved Africans an undercover way of communicating information and enabling them to preserve their cultural heritage, cornrows allowed them to engage in minor acts of rebellion and resistance. In order to convey coded signals, slaves would style their cornrows in various ways. To provide the user with nourishment while fleeing, slaves would also conceal seeds or bold fragments within the braids.
